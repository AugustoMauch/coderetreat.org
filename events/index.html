Global Day Of Coderetreat is a community event happening on November 12 - November 13 2021. Join this year and practice your skills, embrace new paradigms and get to meet new people from all over the world!

We've embraced the virtual events now more than ever. With Covid-19 still prevalent in many locations, we encourage you to stay safe. If an on-site event isn't for you, we hope you'll find a remote event that matches your timezone.

If you're considering organizing your own event, we're here to support you on the journey of organizing your first coderetreat. Head over to the Facilitators Section for Training events, checklists, facilitation videos and more!
